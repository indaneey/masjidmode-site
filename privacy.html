Privacy Policy – Masjid Mode

Last Updated: February 2026

Masjid Mode ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Masjid Mode mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Masjid Mode is designed with privacy in mind. The application does not require account creation and does not collect personal messages, photos, or private user content.

We may collect limited information such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App performance and crash reports
  • Anonymous usage analytics

2. Permissions Usage

Masjid Mode may request certain permissions to provide its core features:

  • Notifications: To silence alerts during prayer or worship.
  • Screen Time / Family Controls: To allow users to voluntarily block distracting applications during prayer sessions.
  • Internet Access: For updates and service functionality.

All restrictions are initiated and controlled by the user. No personal data or notification content is stored externally.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide distraction-free prayer experience
  • Improve application stability and performance
  • Respond to support requests

4. Data Storage

Most application data is stored locally on your device. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

Masjid Mode may use trusted third-party services such as Google Play Billing or Apple services for subscription processing and analytics. These providers operate under their own privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Masjid Mode does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Your Rights

You may stop data collection at any time by uninstalling the application or adjusting device permissions.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
